Vision and mission

the SUE We are Egyptian students at STEM Qena School, and we are deeply concerned about the ongoing environmental and climate challenges facing the world. Among these issues, air pollution poses a significant threat, especially in urban and industrial areas. To address this, we have developed SUE, an innovative air filtration system designed to combat air pollution and improve air quality, contributing to a cleaner and healthier environment for everyone.
